A little bit of good music can go a long way.  When I feel like I'm floundering, it's helpful to throw on some music to get my brain started.  The wheels start turning a little bit faster.  Today I learned that the Lonely Island is my muse.  Weird, right?  After struggling for a while, I got stuck in a YouTube trap, and somehow came out the other end playing Lonely Island videos nonstop and pushed through a project that I've struggled focusing on.  The moral of the story, find ways to get moving.  Inspiration comes in many forms.
